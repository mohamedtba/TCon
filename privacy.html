Privacy Policy for TCon

TCon is a Chrome extension that helps users manage their time on social media. This extension does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information outside the user's browser.

Data Storage

The extension uses chrome.storage.local to save timers and states locally on your device. No data is sent to any server.

Permissions

The extension requests only the storage permission. It does not access your browsing history, cookies, or any sensitive data.
